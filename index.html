About Udaan Institute

A Place Where Potential Meets Purpose

Udaan Institute (formerly Lavinya Institute) is an offline coaching centre in DD Nagar, Gwalior. We believe every child has the ability to soar — they just need the right guidance, discipline, and environment to do so.

Our approach combines strong academic foundations with defence-oriented discipline. Small batch sizes ensure your child receives personal attention — not just another face in a crowded classroom.
