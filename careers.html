Teacher Eligibility

We look for passion, patience, and a genuine love for helping children learn — not just degrees.

✓Bachelor's degree or above in relevant subject(s)
✓Passion for teaching and genuine care for students' progress
✓Patience and ability to explain concepts clearly in simple language
✓Strong command of at least one core subject (Maths, Science, Hindi, English, GK)
✓Punctuality and personal discipline — values we live by at Udaan
✓Good communication with parents — updates and transparency required
