Our Journey

From Lavinya to Udaan

Udaan Institute began as Lavinya Institute — a small coaching centre in DD Nagar, Gwalior, built on one belief: every child deserves a teacher who truly cares.

Over three years, we grew in purpose. We evolved our approach, sharpened our focus on defence-oriented learning, and became Udaan Institute — because "उड़ान" means flight, and that's what we help our students do.

Today: 500+ students taught, 120+ school toppers, and 50+ selections to defence schools across India.

How We Teach

A Teaching Approach That Actually Works

We don't believe in one-size-fits-all education. Here's what makes our classroom different.

🎯

Concept Before Shortcuts

Students truly understand "why" before "how". Strong concepts let them tackle unseen questions with confidence.

📝

Regular Tests & Feedback

Weekly tests identify weak areas early. Honest feedback shared with both students and parents so improvements happen together.

🌅

Morning Routine Discipline

Morning batches for defence aspirants instil the early-rising discipline that Sainik Schools and military academies expect.

👥

Small Groups, Big Attention

Limited students per batch means your child is never invisible. Teachers know every student's strengths and challenges.

📖

Quality Study Material

Handcrafted notes, worksheets, and test series designed specifically for each course — not generic material copied from elsewhere.

🤝

Parent Partnership

Parents are partners in education. Regular updates and open communication ensure every family is informed and involved.
